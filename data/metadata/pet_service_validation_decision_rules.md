# Pet-Service Validation Decision Rules

## Main Model Inclusion

Include only reviewed or high-confidence A-class records in the main service-background layer. Keep B classes for sensitivity or descriptive context.

## Exclusion

Exclude obvious brand/name noise, non-pet animal terms, zoo/aquarium tourism unless explicitly relevant to companion-animal co-presence, and records with no spatially usable evidence.

## Uncertainty

If the record appears pet-related but its function is unclear, keep it out of the main model until second review.

## Publication Boundary

Do not publish raw IDs, exact addresses, exact coordinates, or raw source rows. Public tables should be grid-level aggregated or desensitized.
